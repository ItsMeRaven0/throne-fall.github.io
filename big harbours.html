Big Harbour

The Big Harbour Perk is one of the currently 26 Perks in the game. It is unlocked upon reaching level 28 and increases the amount of fishing ships one harbour can hold. It also increases the health points of harbours.


Description

Fishing Harbours have capacity for 2 additional fishing boats and are more resistant to enemy attacks.

Statistics

Harbours gain +200% health points and can hold 2 more fishing ships for a total of 7.
