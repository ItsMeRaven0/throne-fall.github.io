System Requirements

Currently, Thronefall can only be played on Windows and macOS. This is why there are only system requirements for these 2 operating systems.

These requirements have been taken from Thronefall's Steam page.


Microsoft Windows
  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ i5-9600
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX970
  • Storage: 300 MB available space
  • Sound Card: Just whistle your favourite tune :)
macOS
  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: macOS Ventura or later
  • Processor: M1 or Intel® equivalent
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: M1 or Intel® equivalent
  • Storage: 1 GB available space
  • Sound Card: Just whistle your favourite tune :)
