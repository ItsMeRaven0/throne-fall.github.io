Neuland
Overview

Neuland (German for "New Land") is the Tutorial of Thronefall.
It is one of the 4 levels available in the Early Access version.

It teaches the Player basic mechanics like attacking, building and upgrading.
Neuland is the easiest of the game. As a tutorial, it has only one enemy spawning point located in the west.

Level composition

This level features a small plateau, a ramp leading to the enemy spawn point. Everything is surroundedd by cliffs, a river and a forest.
