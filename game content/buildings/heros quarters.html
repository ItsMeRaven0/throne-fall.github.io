Hero's Quarter
General

Hero's Quarters are a defensive building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice and upon construction the player can select a Hero that will be spawned from the building. The hero is a singular stonger unit and is controlled like any other unit in the game.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible heros once the hero's quarters is constructed. Upgrading the hero's quarters increases the power of the selected hero.
