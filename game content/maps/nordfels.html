Nordfels
Overview

Nordfels (German for "North rock") is the first map of Thronefall after the tutorial.

The map features a large mountain range in the north and a U-shaped river to define the mapborder.

Enemy units spawn from the north-east, east, south-east, south, and the west of the castle with the west, south and northeast of the castle only spawning flying units.


Map composition

Nordfels' Castle layout is split into 3 areas:

  • Outisde area unprotected by walls: This area consists of 3 mills, 3 Watchtowers and 1 archer barracks.
  • The front of the castle: Area with 3 houses, 4 watchtowers and 1 barracks.
  • The inner castle: Area with 7 houses, 3 watchtowers, 2 barracks (1 ranged and 1 melee), and the Castle Center building.
