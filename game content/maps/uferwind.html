Uferwind
Overview

Uferwind is the fifth map of Thronefall. It is one of the 5 maps available in the Early Access version.

With its rugged shoreline to one side and vast open grasslands to the other it offers a challenge unlike any of the maps before. Right from the start you’ll have quite a bit of gold and many building options on your hand, allowing you to explore a variety of different approaches.
