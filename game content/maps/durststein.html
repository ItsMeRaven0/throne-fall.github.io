Durststein
Overview

Durststein (German for "Thirst Stone") is the third map in the game. It is one of the 4 maps available in the Early Access version.

The map features a desert design, 2 new buildings and 2 new enemies. It is also the map with the largest amount of starting money.

Map composition

This map features wide open areas and plenty of ramps and vantage points for ranged units to shoot from. Everything is surrounded by mountains and cliffs, resulting in 4 spawn areas, one in each cardinal direction.
