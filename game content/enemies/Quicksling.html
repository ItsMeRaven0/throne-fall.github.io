Quicksling
General

Quickslings are an advanced siege unit in Thronefall. They deal high amounts of damage, but shoot randomly at all units inside their range.

Description

A unique siege weapon that rapidly fires arrows at random enemies in its range. If you don't have any allied buildings or untis nearby, it will quickly mow you down.
