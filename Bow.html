Bow
General

The bow is the weapon you start with. It deals medium damage at long range.

It's active ability is a knife stab that deals high damage and has a short or long cooldown depending on how much health the attacked target has.

The cooldown is calculated based on the targets current health based on this formular: 7.5sec. * ((0.1 + hpPrecentage) * (1 - 0.1))
