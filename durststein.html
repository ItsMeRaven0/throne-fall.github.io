Durststein
Overview

Durststein (German for "Thirst Stone") is the third level in the game. It is one of the 4 maps available in the Early Access version.

The map features a desert design, 2 new buildings and 2 new enemies. It is also the map with the biggest amount of starting money.

Level composition

This level features wide open areas, plenty of ramps and vantage points for ranged units to shoot from. Everything is surroundedd by mountains and cliffs, resulting in 4 spawn areas, one in each cardianl direction.
