Fortified Houses
General

The Fortified Houses Perk is one of the currently 26 Perks in the game. It is unlocked upon reaching level 35 and icreases the durability of your level 2 houses. Level 2 houses also gain a ranged attack.


Description

Level 2 houses have a lot more health points and shoot arrrows at enemies.

Statistics

Level 2 houses have 50 health points and gain an attack that deals 8 damage.
