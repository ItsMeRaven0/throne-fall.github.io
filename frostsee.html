Frostsee
Overview

Frostsee (German for "Frost lake") is the fourth level in the game. It is one of the 4 maps available in the Early Access version

The map features a snowy tundra design, 2 new buildings, 4 new enemies and a boss. It is also currently the hardest map playable.

Level composition

This level features wide open areas, a few lakes and a buildable outpost. Everything is surrounded by icy mountains and water, resulting in 5 spawn areas, one being an ice cave.
